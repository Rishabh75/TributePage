M S Dhoni - The Legendary Cricketer

  • Mahendra Singh Dhoni is an Indian cricketer who captained the Indian team in limited-overs formats from 11th of September 2007 to 4th of January 2017 and in Test cricket from 2008 to 28th of December 2014
  • Born on 7th July,1981 in place called Ranchi,in state Jharkhand of India .
  • He is currently one of the best wicketkeeper-batsmen in the world. He is right-handed batsman.
  • He debuted for India on 23rd december,2004 versus Bangladesh.
  • He is the most successful captain of India till now. He is also the most successful wicket-keeper in the world now.
  • He is the only Indian captain to bagged all the international ICC trophies under his belt.

Achievements under his belt

  • he has 2 ICC world cups and a Champions Trophy.
  • He helped india to have top spot in the international Test Ranking.
  • He has recived many awards. He has got Padma Shri award, Bharat Ratna Khel Award, ICC player of the year, ICC world ODI and TEST team captain .
  • He also has interest in buisness.He had launched a lifestyle brand named SEVEN in 2016.
